Home Page

Welcome to StuyBS, a site created for Stuyvesant students to replace the disorganized Facebook group of the same name, where students may list, browse, and purchase items for sale by fellow peers!

Please use the menu bar to the left to navigate through the site!

Click on My Listings to see all items you have listed and are available for purchase.

Click on Browse Listings to see all items from sellers and sort them by category, condition, price, etc.

Click on Add A Listing to post a new listing of your own.

Click on Transactions to see all of your past transactions.

Click on Messages to contact other buyers and sellers to discuss and confirm transactions.
